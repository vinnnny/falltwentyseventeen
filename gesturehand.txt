1.  Place the 8 ½” by 11” page vertically in front of you.
2. Draw a square in the middle of the page.
3. Draw another square that is congruent to the square in the middle of the page directly on top of it. 
4. Divide the square on top of the square in the middle of the page into four equal parts.
5. On the middle left side of the square in the middle of the page begin a trapezoid that’s width extends to the bottom of the square and that’s length protrudes 45 degrees and each of the bases of the trapezoid are half of the length of the original square drawn.
